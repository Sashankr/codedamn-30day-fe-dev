About

Go is one of India's leading online travel portals, offering holiday packages in Middle East and Europe. Go offers value for money packages, luxury packages, group departures as well as customized packages.

Go was one of the first aggregators of holiday packages and since 2015 it has provided a forum for agents and holiday package sellers to sell their packages directly on Go website. This has enabled Go to give a wide range of holiday packages including niche and exotic packages to its customers, while providing localised services.
